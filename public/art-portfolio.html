ART PORTFOLIO

My artwork spans concept design, digital illustration, 3D modeling, jewelry making, and graphic design, with a strong focus on visual storytelling for games. I create characters, environments, and objects that explore mood, identity, and worldbuilding, drawing inspiration from interactive media and narrative-driven experiences. Through both original concepts and character studies, my work blends technical skill with imagination to build cohesive, immersive worlds.
