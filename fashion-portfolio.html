FASHION PORTFOLIO

My fashion work explores the intersection of storytelling, material experimentation, and conceptual thinking. From myth and memory to digital worlds, biological innovation, and traditional crafts, each collection investigates transformation, identity, and the tension between beauty and the uncanny. Through silhouette, texture, and detail, I translate narrative and concept into garments that invite viewers to step into immersive, imaginative worlds.
